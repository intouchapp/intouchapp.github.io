Welcome to our Humble Abode.

InTouch started out as an idea we had at Triangle Startup Weekend 2013. It has since grown into a mobile app that allows you to quickly organize and connect with your networks. It features connectivity with your local address book and LinkedIn. Currently supports iOS only.

Who We Are

We're two developers on a mission: Ibraheem Khalifa (@ibraheemk) is a Raleigh native who works for EMC as a software engineer in data analytics. Daniel Hammond (@danielrhammond) lives in Durham and works for Two Toasters a mobile app consulting company.
